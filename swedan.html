Why Choose Swedan?

Sweden leads in research and innovation across various fields, offering students opportunities to participate in cutting-edge research projects alongside leading experts. The country’s stable and prosperous economy provides avenues for internships and practical experience during studies. Sweden also boasts stunning landscapes, including lakes, forests, and mountains.

Popular Courses:

Engineering and Technology, Environmental Science and Sustainability, Design and Arts, Language and Literature, Medicine and Healthcare

Popular Universities:

University of Stockholm, Uppsala University, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, University of Gothenburg, Chalmers University of Technology

Eligibility:

Completion of high school, English proficiency test may be required

Fees Structure:

€7,000 to €20,000 per year
